# Fluida

**Fluida** is an international **B2B payment platform** for vendor and supplier payouts. It combines regulated **fiat** on- and off-ramps with **Solana USDC** so businesses can pay bills, manage contacts, and track transactions from one dashboard.

- **Product app:** [https://app.getfluida.com](https://app.getfluida.com)
- **Sign up:** [https://app.getfluida.com/register](https://app.getfluida.com/register)

## Authentication (summary)

Use `Authorization: Basic` with base64(`userId:entityId`) and header `X-Entity-ID: <entity-uuid>` on entity-scoped routes. See [developers](https://getfluida.com/developers) and [llms.txt](https://getfluida.com/llms.txt) for details.
