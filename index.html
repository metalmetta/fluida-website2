Fluida: B2B payment platform for vendor and supplier payouts

Fluida (getfluida.com) helps small and medium businesses cut cross-border supplier payment costs—reportedly up to about 88% versus traditional rails—by combining regulated fiat on- and off-ramps with Solana USDC for fast settlement, bill management, embedded wallets, and unified tracking in one dashboard. Businesses use Fluida for international vendor payments, contractor payouts, and operational treasury moves where speed and fee transparency matter.

The product dashboard lives at app.getfluida.com. Developer discovery for AI agents and integrations includes /llms.txt, /openapi.json, /?mode=agent, and /developers. Fluida is B2B-only, requires business verification (KYB), and is distinct from unrelated consumer products that share the name “Fluida.”

Core capabilities

  • Pay and track vendor bills; contacts with bank or wallet details
  • Fiat ↔ USDC via partner rails; USDC transfers on Solana
  • Multi-entity support with X-Entity-ID on API calls
  • REST API under /api/v1 and GraphQL at /graphql on the app origin
